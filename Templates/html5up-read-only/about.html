Our History

Founded in 2026, Zephyr began with a simple mission: to provide an easy and reliable root message site for people all over. We believe in Effiecieny, Reliability, and Organization. Our founder Donovan Parker started as a regular boy before being introduced into the world of computer science. Where he fell in love with the concept of email automation.

In the following months Mr. Parker spent all his time learning and studying code and the concept of email automation. He struggled at first and had many drafts for his company. But after building a well balanced team he was able to succeed in his goal in creating Zephyr.
